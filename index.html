Kawasaki Motorcycles

For over 50 years, Kawasaki Motorcycles are manufactured by the Motorcycle and Engine division of Kawasaki Heavy Industries at plants in Japan, Michigan, Philippines, India, Indonesia, Bangladesh and Thailand. For many years, Kawasaki has been the world's leader in quality motorcycle production and has been millions of customers go-to due to their consistent top-of-the-line production (Wikipedia). I can personally attest to the marvelous products made over at Kawasaki. I have personally owned, ridden, and raced over thirteen Kawasaki Motorcycles, including streetbikes, dirtbikes, and quads. The company's quality of product and customer service is astounding and I am proud to be a part of the Kawi family.
